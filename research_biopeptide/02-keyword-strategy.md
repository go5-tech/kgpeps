## Geo-Targeting Rules

- Add "india" or "in india" to **every title tag** where natural
- Add "india" to **product URL slugs** (e.g., `/shop/bpc-157-peptide-india/`)
- Use INR pricing prominently on all product pages
- Mention India-based shipping, fast delivery in product descriptions
- Target city-level keywords in blog posts if scaling: "buy peptides Mumbai", "peptides Delhi", "peptides Bangalore"
