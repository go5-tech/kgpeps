## Content Rules

1. Never dump all posts on the same date — publish 2-3 per week
2. Every post must link to at least 2 product pages
3. Every post needs a unique meta description (not auto-generated)
4. Add FAQ schema to every post via RankMath
5. Include "india" naturally in the first 100 words
6. Add CoA/lab report mention in every product deep-dive
7. Update top posts with "2026" or "2027" in title when year rolls over
