A distinctive GHRP-6 mechanism is strong appetite stimulation. GHRP-6 signals hunger through multiple pathways—ghrelin mimicry in the brain, gastric signaling, and hypothalamic appetite centers.

Unlike ipamorelin, GHRP-6 raises cortisol. This is significant: cortisol is catabolic (breaks down tissue). Elevated cortisol opposes muscle building.
