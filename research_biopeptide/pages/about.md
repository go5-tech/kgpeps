## Our Quality Standards

Quality isn't a feature at Peptide Central—it's our foundation. Every peptide we sell meets these standards:

**HPLC Testing:** Each batch is analyzed using High-Performance Liquid Chromatography, the gold standard for purity verification. We verify identity, purity percentage, and detect any impurities down to 0.01%.

**99.9% Purity Guaranteed:** All our products are 99.9% pure or higher. This means you're getting the intended compound, not a mixture of byproducts and contaminants that could confound your research.

**Third-Party Verification:** We partner with independent, accredited laboratories to test our peptides. This removes any conflict of interest and provides neutral, trustworthy verification.

**Certificate of Analysis with Every Order:** Your COA documents the peptide identity, purity percentage, batch number, testing date, and lab credentials. Transparency is non-negotiable.

**Rigorous Storage & Handling:** We maintain cold-chain logistics, vacuum-sealed packaging, and proper storage conditions (2-8°C) to ensure peptides arrive stable and uncompromised.

**Regulatory Compliance:** All peptides are imported and distributed in compliance with Indian pharmaceutical and customs regulations. Every product carries "For Research Purposes Only" labeling.
