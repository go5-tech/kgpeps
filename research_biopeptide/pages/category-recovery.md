## Handling and Storage Guidelines

All recovery peptides require proper handling to maintain stability and purity:

**Storage:** Lyophilized peptides should be stored at 2–8°C (refrigerator) or -20°C (freezer) for long-term stability.

**Reconstitution:** Use sterile bacteriostatic water (0.9% sodium chloride solution). Typical reconstitution concentration is 1–5 mg/mL depending on research application.

**Stability after reconstitution:** Reconstituted solutions are stable for 14–30 days at 2–8°C if properly sealed.
