# Backlink Plan: Buy Peptides India

Goal: build clean, relevant authority for `https://peptidecentral.in/` around the `buy peptides India` topic without hidden text, spam comments, link farms, or exact-match anchor abuse.

## Week 1

1. Submit 5 guest post pitches from `backlink-prospects.csv`.
2. Use only educational topics around COA, HPLC verification, storage, and supplier comparison.
3. Track each outreach in the CSV `status` column.
4. Keep anchors mostly branded:
   - 50% `Peptide Central` or `peptidecentral.in`
   - 25% partial anchors like `buy peptides online in India`
   - 15% product anchors like `BPC-157 in India`
   - 10% naked URL

## Week 2

1. Follow up with non-responders.
2. Pitch 5 more sites.
3. Answer 3 relevant Reddit/forum threads only if the thread asks for peptide source or quality verification help.
4. Do not drop links in unrelated supplement discussions.

## Week 3

1. Publish or submit first two guest posts.
2. Build one link to homepage and one link to `/products/` or a product page.
3. Start product-specific outreach for:
   - BPC-157
   - TB-500
   - GHK-Cu
   - Retatrutide

## Week 4

1. Review links that went live.
2. Check Google Search Console for impressions on:
   - buy peptides india
   - buy peptides online india
   - bpc 157 india
   - tb 500 india
   - ghk cu india
3. Continue with the sites that actually respond.

## Rules

- Do not buy bulk backlink packages.
- Do not use hidden links or cloaked pages.
- Do not overuse exact-match anchor text.
- Do not use spun articles.
- Do not post undisclosed promotional Reddit comments.
- Prefer fewer relevant links over many low-quality links.
